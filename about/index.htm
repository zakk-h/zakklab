About

Welcome to my page. Here I post various content relating to Valdese, such community interviews, Rotary meetings and recordings, videos of local features like McGalliard Falls, Childrens' Park, the Old Rock School, Valdese Splash Pad, and Valdese Lakeside Park, among others. I host the Valdese Lakeside Park interactive app with location tracking and trail and map overlays, along with documentation on it, the Wildlife page tracking all videos recorded on my trail cameras, along with information on points of interest in Valdese Lakeside Park.
